Cloud

Microsoft Office 365is an easy-to-use set of web-enabled tools that let you access your email, important documents, contacts, and calendar from almost anywhere and any device-including PCs, Macintosh computers, iPhones, Android phones, and BlackBerry smartphones. Designed to meet the needs of a diverse range of small businesses, Office 365 lets you choose which tools to use;

  • Email, Calendar, Anti Virus/Spam & personal Archiving
  • Collaboration Portal
  • Virtual Meetings
  • IM & Video Calls
  • Office web apps
  • Office professional plus
  • Voicemail & advanced archiving
  • Access/Visio/Excel services
  • Voice

Private Cloud: A private cloud is a scalable cloud environmentproviding the agility and efficiency of a public cloudbuilt on infrastructure dedicated exclusively for your organisation. It can be hosted in your own data center or in a partner data center. Private clouds are ideal when you need to accelerate innovation, have large compute and storage requirements, or have very strict control, security, and compliance needs.

XaaS or Anything-as-a-Service, refers to the growing diversity of services available over the Internet via cloud computing as opposed to being provided locally, or on premises. Also known as Everything-as-a-Service, Anything-as-a-Service reflects the vast potential for on-demand cloud services and is already being rapidly adopted across various scenarios including;

  • Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)
  • Backup-as-a-Service
  • Desktop-as-a-Service (DaaS),
  • DisasterRecovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS)
  • Network-as-a-Service (NaaS)
  • Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS)
  • Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS)
